Canadian Association for Medical Education (CAME)

Create a modern, immersive, academic-grade website for the Canadian Association for Medical Education (CAME).

The site should resemble the design quality and professionalism of:

Harvard Medical School

Stanford Medicine

AAMC

UBC Faculty of Medicine

Johns Hopkins Medicine

The design must communicate:

Academic authority

National leadership

Professional credibility

Collaboration and innovation

The site should feel clean, modern, and prestigious, not corporate or commercial.

PRIMARY GOALS OF THE WEBSITE

The website must focus on four primary outcomes:

Increase CAME Membership

Promote leadership and education programs

Build a national community of medical educators

Encourage donations and collaboration

Key user actions:

Become a member

Register for programs

Apply for awards or grants

Donate

Subscribe to updates

Explore professional development

TYPOGRAPHY

Use a modern academic pairing.

Headings:

Inter / Source Sans / IBM Plex Sans

Body:

Clean sans serif optimized for readability.

Typography scale:

Hero Title: 64px–72px Page Title: 42px Section Title: 32px Subsection: 22px Body text: 16–18px

Spacing should be generous and breathable.

Avoid dense academic blocks.
